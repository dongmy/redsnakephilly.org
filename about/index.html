About RedSnakePhilly

The RedSnake is a meeting where Rubyists and Pythonistas get together to throw down some hard tech and see how we measure up.

The format is 2 hours of alternating lightning talks. It's intense, technical and mind blowing.

Location

The event is held at 3730 Walnut St in Huntsman Hall at The Wharton School of Univerity of Pennsylvania.
