About RedSnakePhilly

The RedSnake is a meeting where Rubyists and Pythonistas get together to throw down some hard tech and see how we measure up. Ultimately we aim to accomplish the following:

  1. To be the premier technical event for hardcore programmers in Philadelphia
  2. To inspire and provide an environment of learning to those who are new to programming
  3. To introduce Philadelphia's top companies to Philadelphia's top programmers
  4. To raise national recognition for Philadelphia's tech community and attract top talent to the city

The format is 2 hours of alternating 10 minute lightning talks. It's intense, technical, and mind blowing.

When

RedSnakePhilly is on February 21st, 2012. Doors open at 6pm. See the schedule for more details as they develop.

Location

The event will be held at Liberty Two (50 South 16th St) on the 32nd floor in the conference rooms of Buchanan Ingersoll & Rooney PC.

Transportation

For drivers, grab some google directions and see the section below on parking.

For public transit, Liberty Two is close to a number of buses, Suburban Station and both the Market-Frankford and Broad St Subways. Google's transit directions should get you there just fine.

Parking

There are a number of Central Parking garages in the area that can provide parking for about $10-$20. Be sure to print a coupon before hand to get the lower rate advertised on the website.
